# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Travel Services Limited (TSL) Redesign

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** TSL 2026 Digital Transformation  
**Client:** Travel Services Limited (Papua New Guinea)  
**Objective:** Redesign the existing web presence into a premium, high-converting, and SEO-optimized platform that reinforces TSL's position as PNG's leading travel agency.

## 2. Goals & Success Metrics
*   **Modernization:** Replace the legacy UI with a "Tropical Luxury" aesthetic.
*   **Conversion:** Increase lead generation through strategic CTA placement and streamlined inquiry forms.
*   **Authority:** Showcase 20+ years of local expertise and global partnerships.
*   **UX/UI:** Achieve a seamless, responsive experience across mobile and desktop.
*   **SEO:** Improve search visibility through semantic HTML and optimized content hierarchy.

## 3. Target Audience
*   **Corporate Clients:** Business leaders and logistics managers requiring reliable, high-touch travel management in PNG.
*   **Leisure Travelers:** Domestic and international tourists seeking curated, high-end, or adventurous PNG experiences.
*   **Government & NGO:** Organizations requiring complex logistics and group movement support.

## 4. Site Architecture & Sitemap
1.  **Home:** High-impact hero, service overview, trust signals, and quick inquiry.
2.  **Corporate Travel:** Targeted solutions for managed travel, charter, and VIP services.
3.  **Leisure Packages:** Experiential storytelling, curated tour cards (Coastal Luxury, Cultural, Adventure).
4.  **Our Services:** Comprehensive grid of all offerings (Flights, Hotels, Cruises, Insurance).
5.  **About Us:** Founder's story, vision, mission, and core expertise pillars.
6.  **Contact Us:** (Pending) Global locations, departmental contacts, and lead capture.

## 5. Key Features & Functionality
### A. Universal Navigation & Footer
*   **Sticky Header:** "Book Now" primary CTA, mega-menu for services.
*   **SEO Footer:** Comprehensive link structure, newsletter sign-up, and trust badges.

### B. Lead Capture System
*   **Contextual Forms:** Tailored inquiry forms for Corporate (volume-based) vs. Leisure (interest-based).
*   **Quick Inquiry:** Bottom-of-page lead capture on the Home screen.

### C. Experience Discovery
*   **Dynamic Tour Cards:** Package metadata including duration, price from, and category tags.
*   **Service Pillars:** Icon-driven navigation for quick access to specific travel products.

## 6. Design System Specifications
*   **Core Palette:** Deep Ocean Blue (#002b49), Vibrant Turquoise, and Neutral Surfaces (#f8f9fa).
*   **Typography:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Clean, modern, highly readable).
*   **Visual Style:** Rounded corners (8px), subtle elevations, high-quality photography, and generous whitespace.

## 7. Technical Requirements
*   **Frontend:** Semantic HTML5, CSS Grid/Flexbox, responsive breakpoints (Mobile-first).
*   **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance (contrast, touch targets, aria-labels).
*   **SEO:** H1-H6 hierarchy, schema markup for local business/travel agency, optimized image alt-text.
*   **Performance:** Lazy loading for high-res imagery, minimized layout shift (CLS).

## 8. Content Strategy
*   **Tone of Voice:** Authoritative, Professional, Inspiring, and Trustworthy.
*   **Visuals:** High-impact, authentic PNG landscapes and professional travel contexts.
